TranzAlpine Train Tours and Tickets

The TranzAlpine Train is New Zealand’s most spectacular train journey, one which winds its way through the dramatic gorges and alpine forests of the Southern Alps, over staggering viaducts and dizzying mountain passes. From Christchurch on the East Coast to Greymouth on the West Coast, this almost 5-hour trip serves up endless photo opportunities as it runs through the heart of the South Island.

Q:How much does it cost to visit the TranzAlpine Train in 2024?
A:

Depending on what type of ride you book and during what season, you can book a TranzAlpine Train ticket for as little as US$156 for a regular standard Scenic ticket, but it can also run you as high as US$500 if you’re planning to upgrade to the Scenic Plus ticket option.

Q:What is the TranzAlpine Train, and what is its route?
A:

The TranzAlpine Train is a scenic railway journey in New Zealand that traverses the Southern Alps between Christchurch and Greymouth. It's often touted as one of the world’s best train journeys and the route takes passengers past magnificent South Island landscapes, including mountain lakes, river gorges, and forests.

Q:How long does the TranzAlpine Train journey take?
A:

The TranzAlpine Train journey typically takes about 4.5–5 hours to travel coast-to-coast across New Zealand's South Island. Covering approximately 139 miles (223 kilometers) between Christchurch and Greymouth, this world-renowned picturesque journey boasts spectacular scenery and a year-round schedule, offering passengers unforgettable vistas of mountain landscapes and lush forests.

Q:Are food and drinks available on the TranzAlpine Train?
A:

Yes, food and drink are available to purchase onboard the TranzAlpine Train. There’s an onboard Scenic Cafe which serves a variety of different dishes, as well as a fully licensed bar. Sample dishes include a vegetarian pie, lamb shank, chicken salad, and a variety of cakes, snacks, and baked goods.

Q:What is the best time of year to take the TranzAlpine Train for optimal views?
A:

The TranzAlpine Train offers spectacular views year-round, but many believe the best time to experience it is during the winter months. It’s during this time that the mountains are capped with snow and the landscapes are transformed into a winter wonderland, adding an extra layer of beauty to the journey.
