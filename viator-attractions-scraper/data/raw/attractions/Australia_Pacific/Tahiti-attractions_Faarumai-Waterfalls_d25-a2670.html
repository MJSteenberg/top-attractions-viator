Faarumai Waterfalls Tours and Tickets

There are waterfalls all around Tahiti, but the most popular and accessible are the three waterfalls at Faarumai, also known as the Cascades of Faarumai. The three majestic falls are surrounded by tropical vegetation and are more easily reachable than other waterfall sites on the island. Although you may see visitors taking a dip in the pools, swimming here was outlawed after someone was injured.
