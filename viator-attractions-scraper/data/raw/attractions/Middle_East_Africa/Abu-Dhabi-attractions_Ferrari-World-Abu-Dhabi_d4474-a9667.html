Ferrari World Abu Dhabi Tours and Tickets

21,804 reviews

Set on Yas Island in Abu Dhabi, the race car–themed Ferrari World features 20 rides and attractions—everything from the toddler-friendly carousel of Ferrari prototype cars to cutting-edge racing simulators that will please older kids and teens. The largest indoor theme park in the world is also home to the world’s fastest roller coaster, the Formula Rossa, a hydraulic-powered thrill ride that sees visitors strapped into a Ferrari Formula One-like coaster car and launched at speeds of up to 150 miles (240 kilometers) per hour.

  • Open daily, 11am - 8pm
  • Yas Island Drive, Abu Dhabi

Q:What not to miss in Ferrari World Abu Dhabi?
A:
For most visitors, the absolute must-do is the Formula Rossa, the world's fastest roller coaster; it hits 75mph (120km/h) in 4.9 seconds. Beyond that, it’s sort of up to your personal preference—choose to walk on Ferrari World’s roof, race to change a tire, soar down the drop tower, tour a historic Ferrari factory, and more.
Q:How much time is needed in Ferrari World Abu Dhabi?
A:
Most visitors want to get their fill of Ferrari World Abu Dhabi, as this can be a once-in-a-lifetime experience. As such, you’ll probably want an entire day. With that amount of time, you’ll be able to experience the roller coasters, add on an experience like the zipline, and check off everything on your list.
Q:What is special about Ferrari World Abu Dhabi?
A:
Ferrari World Abu Dhabi is the world's first Ferrari-themed park. But perhaps more importantly, it’s home to the tallest loop ride and the fastest roller coaster in the world—it reaches 75mph (120km/h) in under five seconds. There are plenty of other things to do here, from family and children’s rides to tire-changing challenges and beyond.
Q:Are all rides free in Ferrari World Abu Dhabi?
A:
Your entry ticket into Ferrari World Abu Dhabi includes most things to do inside the park, including the rides. A few attractions are excluded, like the Karting Academy, the Roof Walk, and the Driving Experience. But if you’re here for the roller coasters, you’re covered.
Q:Is there a dress code for Ferrari World Abu Dhabi?
A:
Yes, there is a dress code for Ferrari World Abu Dhabi. For both men and women (and children, regardless of age), your shoulders and knees should be covered with clothing that isn’t see-through. That will keep your appearance under local laws and etiquette. Luckily, Ferrari World is mostly indoors, and there’s air conditioning.
Q:How many rides are at Ferrari World Abu Dhabi?
A:
As of this writing, there are more than 40 rides and attractions at Ferrari World Abu Dhabi. That number includes the world’s fastest roller coaster, the tallest loop ride, and the tallest space-frame structure ever built on the planet. Of course, if you’re not looking for thrills, there are also family-friendly rides, go-karts, and more.
