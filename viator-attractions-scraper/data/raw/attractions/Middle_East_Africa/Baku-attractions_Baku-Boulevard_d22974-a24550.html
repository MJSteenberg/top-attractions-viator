Baku Boulevard (Denizkenari Milli Park) Tours and Tickets

The bright and breezy Baku Boulevard (Denizkenari Milli Park) boasts a wide swath of sidewalks lined with trees, shops, and al fresco cafés. It extends 3 miles (5 kilometers) along the seafront from National Flag Square beyond Freedom Square. Highlights of the boulevard include the 197-foot (60-meter) Baku Ferris Wheel and the Park Bulvar shopping mall.
