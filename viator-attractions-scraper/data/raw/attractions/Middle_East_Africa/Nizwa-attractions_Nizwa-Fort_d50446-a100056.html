Nizwa Fort Tours and Tickets

One of Oman’s largest forts, Nizwa Fort stands in the country’s former capital, Nizwa, overlooking a date-palm oasis and the arid Hajar Mountains. Built in the 17th century by the Ya’rubi tribes who expelled the country’s Portuguese occupiers, the fort draws visitors with its massive cylindrical tower—Oman’s tallest—and its beautiful mud-brick architecture and rampart views.
