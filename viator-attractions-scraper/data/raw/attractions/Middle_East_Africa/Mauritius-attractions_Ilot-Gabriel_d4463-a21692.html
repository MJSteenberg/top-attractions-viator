Ilot Gabriel Tours and Tickets

One of Mauritius’ Northern Islands, the small and uninhabited Ilot Gabriel (Gabriel Island) offers paradise on an accessible scale. Notable for its white-sand beaches, surrounding coral reef, and crystal-clear waters, it’s a popular place to snorkel, swim, and sunbathe. The island also is a nature reserve, and bird watchers may spy its resident long-tailed seabird, the paille-en-queue.
