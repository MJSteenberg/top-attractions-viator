Ain Dubai Tours and Tickets

11,407 reviews

The Ain Dubai observation wheel soars 820 feet (250 meters) above sea level on Bluewaters Island, located on the southwest side of Dubai’s Palm Jumeirah archipelago. Opened in 2021, it’s one of the world’s highest observation wheels and treats passengers to rotating views of the Dubai skyline and Persian Gulf.
