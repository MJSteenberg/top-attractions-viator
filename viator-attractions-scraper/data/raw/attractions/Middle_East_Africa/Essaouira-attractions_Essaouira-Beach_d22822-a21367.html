Essaouira Beach (Plage d'Essaouira) Tours and Tickets

Lined with bars, restaurants, and surf shops, Essaouira Beach (Plage d'Essaouira) is a Moroccan hot spot for surfers, windsurfers, and kitesurfers, thanks to its steady, year-round winds. The town has a charming hippie atmosphere, and travelers who are not indulging in water sports enjoy horse, camel, or quad rides along the broad sandy beach.

Q:How much does it cost to visit Essaouira Beach in 2024?
A:

There is no charge to visit Essaouira Beach, however, if you're coming from Marrakech, you'll need to pay for transportation. Day tours start at around US$20 per person and typically include round-trip transportation from your hotel. Beachfront activities cost extra, such as surfing lessons that start at around US$35 per person.

Q:What is the best month to visit Essaouira Beach?
A:

Although Essaouira Beach is a year-round destination, the best time to visit is during the JuneAugust summer season, when there's usually less wind. June is a particularly good time to visit the town of Essaouira, as the annual Gnaoua World Music Festival takes place.

Q:How do I get to Essaouira Beach?
A:

Essaouira Beach is within easy walking distance from the center of Essaouira, which is about 112 miles (180 kilometers) west of Marrakech. Regular bus services connect to Essaouira from Marrakech's Bab Doukkala bus station, but seats can fill up in advance. Same-day private and small-group tours from Marrakech are a convenient option.

Q:What watersports can be done at Essaouira Beach?
A:

Surfing is the most popular water sport at Essaouira Beach; many visitors come to either learn how to surf or perfect their skills. You can also kitesurf, swim, sunbathe, go on a camel ride, or sign up for an ATV adventure.

Q:Is visiting Essaouira Beach worth it?
A:

Yes, visiting Essaouira Beach is worth it, especially if youre a fan of surfing. Its also fun to take a walk on the sands as the sun begins to set into the Atlantic. Even if you dont go to the beach, the town of Essaouira is worth visiting for its pretty architecture and great shopping.
