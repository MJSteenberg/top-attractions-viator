Tomb of Ramses III Tours and Tickets

Extending for around 125 meters (410 feet), the Tomb of Ramses III—also known as KV 11— is among the longest and best-preserved tombs in the Valley of the Kings on Luxor's West Bank. The interior walls are adorned with hieroglyphs and colorful bas-relief art depicting scenes from ancient funerary texts.
