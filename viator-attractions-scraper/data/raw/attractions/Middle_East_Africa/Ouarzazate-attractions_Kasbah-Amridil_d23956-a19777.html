Kasbah Amridil Tours and Tickets

533 reviews

For those seeking an authentic kasbah experience, look no further than Amridil. This 17th-century destination is a well-preserved and expertly restored structure that provides beautiful vistas of the surrounding landscape, including the magnificent Skoura palm groves. It's a rare opportunity to step back in time and immerse oneself in a centuries-old way of life.

  • Oulad Yaacoub, Skoura, 45500
