Kasbah of Aït Ben Haddou (Ksar of Ait Benhaddou) Tours and Tickets

29,108 reviews

The UNESCO-listed Kasbah of Aït Ben Haddou (Ksar of Ait Benhaddou) is one of Morocco’s most impressive historic landmarks and a popular film location for Hollywood movies. Sculpted from traditional mud bricks and fortified by walls of dark red pisé, this kasbah lies on the old trans-Saharan trade route, at the border of the High Atlas Mountains and Sahara Desert.

Q:What is the history of Kasbah of Ait Ben Haddou (Ksar of Ait Benhaddou)?
A:
The site of Kasbah of Ait Ben Haddou (Ksar of Ait Benhaddou) dates back to the 11th century, but most buildings were built after the 17th and housed Berber families in a fortified tribal village style. The Berber architecture has been preserved, and a few families still live here.
Q:What is the best way to get to Kasbah of Ait Ben Haddou (Ksar of Ait Benhaddou)?
A:
It’s best to hire a car and driver or book a tour to visit the Kasbah of Ait Ben Haddou (Ksar of Ait Benhaddou). There’s no public transport to the kasbah, which is about a three-and-a-half-hour drive from Marrakech or a 30-minute drive from Ouarzazate.
Q:Is there a fee to visit Kasbah of Ait Ben Haddou (Ksar of Ait Benhaddou)?
A:
No, there isn’t a fee to visit the Kasbah of Ait Ben Haddou (Ksar of Ait Benhaddou). Sometimes, touts will be waiting around outside, asking you to buy a ticket. Alas, they are just out to make a buck, and no, you don’t need to purchase an entrance ticket.
Q:What tours go to Kasbah of Ait Ben Haddou (Ksar of Ait Benhaddou)?
A:
Many tours run to Kasbah of Ait Ben Haddou (Ksar of Ait Benhaddou), so most travelers will have an opinion. Choose a one-day tour from Marrakech and tack on a tour of the Hollywood hotspot, Ouarzazate. Some tours include camel rides, meals, or even overnight stays in the Sahara.
Q:When is the best time to visit Kasbah of Ait Ben Haddou (Ksar of Ait Benhaddou)?
A:
Aim for sunset for the best time to visit Kasbah of Ait Ben Haddou (Ksar of Ait Benhaddou), since the orange and red light is perfect for some beautiful photos. If you can’t make it for sunset, try for early morning when the light is also soft.
Q:Is the Kasbah of Ait Ben Haddou (Ksar of Ait Benhaddou) worth it?
A:
Yes, this impressive fortress is worth a visit, especially if you’re touring the Ouarzazate and the surrounding areas. Wandering around the kasbah feels like you’re going back in time to a different era, and the angles of light at sunset are magical. Do pay a visit if you can while you’re in Morocco.
