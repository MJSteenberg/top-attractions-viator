Kejetia Market Tours and Tickets

This teeming open-air marketplace in the heart of Kumasi is often called the largest in West Africa. Whether or not that’s been proven, you can see where the description comes from: There are around 12,000 stalls open for business, selling everything from clothes and Ashanti sandals to fresh produce and hand-made beads. But don’t expect an attraction geared toward tourists. This is where locals get their shopping done.
