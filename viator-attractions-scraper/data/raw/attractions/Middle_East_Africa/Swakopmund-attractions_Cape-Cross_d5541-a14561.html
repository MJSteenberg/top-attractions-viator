Cape Cross Tours and Tickets

255 reviews

The rocky shores of Cape Cross seem to be constantly moving–this long stretch of Atlantic coastline is home to thousands of Cape Fur seals who spend their days playing, fighting, and lazing on the rocks. The nature reserve teems with diverse life; apart from the seals, you might spot jackals, flamingos, and other bird life.
