Birkat al Mouz Ruins (Harat Al Siybani) Tours and Tickets

1,126 reviews

Set under Oman’s Jebel Akhdar mountains above the village of the same name, the Birkat al Mouz Ruins (Harat al Siybani) offer a snapshot into old Omani life. Here, collapsed mudbrick houses and a UNESCO-protected falaj irrigation channel climb to a crumbled watchtower with sweeping views over the ruins, palms, and village below.
