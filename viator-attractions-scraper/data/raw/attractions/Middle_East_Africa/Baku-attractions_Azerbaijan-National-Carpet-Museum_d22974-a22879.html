Azerbaijan National Carpet Museum (Azerbaycan Milli Xalça Muzeyi) Tours and Tickets

811 reviews

The museum rolls out the red carpet for its extensive collection of Azerbaijani carpets. They span centuries, materials, and weaving techniques, from the 17th century to the present day. Carpets saved from the Susha Museum of History and thousands of traditional (sometimes ancient) everyday items showcase local culture and history.

  • 28 Mikayıl Hüseynov Ave., Baku, 1000
