Al Badiyah Mosque Tours and Tickets

Located in the coastal village of Al Bidya, Al Badiyah Mosque (Al Bidyah Mosque) is the oldest standing mosque in the United Arab Emirates. Constructed of stone and mud, it features an unusual design of four irregular-shaped and layered domes. Thought to date from the 1400s, it remains a functioning mosque to this day.

Fujairah holds a unique position in the UAE. It is the only emirate that lies on the eastern side of the UAE, along the Gulf of Oman, while other six emirates are along the Arabian Gulf. Khor Fakkan is a city and exclave of the Emirate of Sharjah, located on the east coast of the United Arab Emirates, facing the Gulf of Oman, and geographically surrounded by the Emirate of Fujairah. The city, the second largest on the east coast after Fujairah City, is set on the bay of Khor Fakkan, which means &#34;Creek of Two Jaws&#34;. The Emirate derives its name from a spring of water located beneath one of the mountains. The Hajar mountain range that divides the UAE in two, from Ras Al Khaimah to Al Ain has kept Fujairah separated from the rest of the country. The historical importance of Fujairah dates back to the period before the birth of Christ. It was known in the old ages as the land of sea giants.
