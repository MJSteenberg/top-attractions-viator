Sheikh Faisal Bin Qassim Al Thani Museum (FBQ Museum) Tours and Tickets

88 reviews
Free admission

A privately-owned museum showcasing Sheikh Faisal’s collection of Islamic and Qatari cultural artifacts, the Sheikh Faisal Bin Qassim Al Thani Museum (FBQ Museum) offers visitors insight into Qatar’s heritage. The museum, in the village of Al Samriya outside Doha, is hosted inside a fort home to stables and an oryx sanctuary.

  • Al Samariyah, Doha
