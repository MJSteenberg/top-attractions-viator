Nairobi National Museum Tours and Tickets

With a massive permanent collection that combines history, culture, and artwork, the Nairobi National Museum is a must for travelers interested in Kenya’s rich heritage. Artifacts are displayed across two floors, and a nature trail winds through the surrounding grounds, a botanical garden, and collections of outdoor sculptures.

Q:How much does it cost to visit Nairobi National Museum in 2024?
A:

Visiting the Nairobi National Museum costs 1,200 Kenyan shillings per person. Teens and children 16 or younger get half off. Citizens of Kenya and East African residents pay 200 and 600 Kenyan shillings, respectively. Foreign visitors can visit nearby Snake Park with bundled admission for 1,500 Kenyan shillings.

Q:What is the Nairobi National Museum known for?
A:

One of the Kenyan capital’s main attractions, this historic museum (more than a century old) showcases the country’s history, culture, and natural heritage. Exhibits span a range of topics, from ethnographic displays featuring textiles and tools to contemporary art, ecology, and wildlife. It is located on Museum Hill.

Q:What is the best month to visit the Nairobi National Museum?
A:

Generally speaking, the best time to visit Nairobi is during Kenya’s dry seasons, from January to March and from June through October. However, the museum is open year-round, and, since it’s indoors, it is a great location for a rainy-day activity.

Q:Can you go to the Nairobi National Museum on your own?
A:

Yes, the museum can be visited independently. You don’t need an escort or guide to access the exhibits. However, consider coming with a guide for more context on Kenya’s history, culture, art, and ecology. The Nairobi National Museum is included in many tours of the capital.

Q:Is visiting the Nairobi National Museum worth it?
A:

Yes. A visit to the Nairobi National Museum is especially worthwhile at the start of your vacation. The museum gives an overview of Kenya’s diversity and history, which can enrich your visit, whether Nairobi is your final destination or the jumping-off point for a safari or beach resort vacation.
