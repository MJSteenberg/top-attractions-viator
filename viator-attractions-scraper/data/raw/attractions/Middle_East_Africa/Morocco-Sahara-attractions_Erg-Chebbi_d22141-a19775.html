Erg Chebbi Tours and Tickets

The Erg Chebbi is a majestic sand sea in Morocco’s desert that spans about 17 miles (28 kilometers) and boasts dunes that reach heights of about 525 feet (160 meters). Conveniently near the town of Merzouga, this is the Moroccan Sahara’s most popular desert camping destination.

Q:How much does it cost to visit Erg Chebbi in 2024?
A:

There are several ways to experience Erg Chebbi; options range from sunset dinners, ATV rides, overnight stays in desert camps, and multi-day tours. Group dinners range from around US$20, ATV rides are about US$50, while camel trekking with overnight tours start at US$55 and higher, depending on how luxurious your camp is or if your tour is private.

Q:When is the best time to visit Erg Chebbi?
A:

You can visit Erg Chebbi all year round, but keep in mind that it is very hot in the summer months, especially July and August. It does get cool in the winter, so bring layers, especially if you’re staying overnight. Spring and fall tend to be the most popular times to visit.

Q:Can you visit Erg Chebbi by yourself or do you need a guided tour?
A:

While you can visit Erg Chebbi by yourself, tours offer a convenient option, as most include transportation from Marrakech and various activities. Arranging taxis by yourself can be challenging.

Q:How long do I need to spend in Erg Chebbi?
A:

Either head out to Erg Chebbi for a half-day of adventure with ATVs and camel rides, or add on a sunset dinner for a full day of exploring. If you choose an overnight stay, you’ll get to enjoy both sunset and sunrise over the dunes.

Q:Is visiting Erg Chebbi worth it?
A:

Yes, visiting Erg Chebbi is worth it—Morocco has a varied landscape and you won’t want to miss the desert side. If visiting from Marrakech, Erg Chebbi offers a chance to experience Bedouin life and hospitality with camel rides and cups of green tea in the desert.
