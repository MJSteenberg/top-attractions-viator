Manhyia Palace Museum (Asantehene's Palace) Tours and Tickets

352 reviews

Manhyia Palace Museum offers a glimpse into Ashanti royal history and culture. Once home to Ashanti kings, the museum features an extensive collection of royal artifacts, including swords, jewelry, medals, and the revered Golden Stool. You can explore the palace's interiors and learn about the kingdom's past, making it an essential destination for understanding the Ashanti cultural legacy.
