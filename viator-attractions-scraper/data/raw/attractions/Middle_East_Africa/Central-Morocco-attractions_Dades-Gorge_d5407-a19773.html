Dades Gorge (Gorges du Dades) Tours and Tickets

10,564 reviews

Carved over millions of years by the Dadès River, the rugged red valleys known as the Dadès Gorge (Gorges du Dadès wind their way between the Atlas and Anti Atlas Mountains. The Tamellalt Valley boasts weird and wonderful rock formations, like melted candles; a notorious strip of blacktop loops and twirls down from Boumalne du Dadès.
