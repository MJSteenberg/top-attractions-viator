Kaseya Center Tours and Tickets

4,759 reviews

Home to the NBA’s Miami Heat, Kaseya Center (formerly the American Airlines Arena and Miami-Dade Arena) is a must for sports fans. Open since 1999, the arena is also a major concert venue, having hosted the likes of Madonna, U2, and Mariah Carey. Designed by the architecture firms Arquitectonica and 360 Architecture, the arena on Biscayne Bay is also known for its striking modernist appearance.

  • 601 Biscayne Blvd., Miami, Florida, 33132
