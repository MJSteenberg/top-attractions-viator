Miracle Mile Shops at Planet Hollywood Tours and Tickets

The Las Vegas Strip’s Miracle Mile Shops at Planet Hollywood Resort & Casino comprises more than one mile (1.5 kilometers) of shops, restaurants, and live entertainment venues. Stores include big brands such as lululemon, Sephora, and H&M, and the centrally-located street offers respite from the casino floor.
