Alexandria Christ Church Tours and Tickets

At Christ Church in Old Town Alexandria, Virginia, visitors can sit in the same pews that George Washington and Robert E. Lee once worshipped. Commenced in 1767, the church is a living testament to American history, as is the site's cemetery, which memorializes 34 Confederate prisoners of war who perished in prison camps during the Civil War.
