Quiraing Tours and Tickets

6,011 reviews

Quiraing is a hiking trail on the Isle of Skye in northern Scotland. It's a 4-mile (7-kilometer) loop that passes through some spectacular Scottish landscapes. The trail is part of the Trotternish Ridge, which was formed by a landslide that created cliffs, plateaus, and rock pinnacles. You can soak up coastal views and see many unique land formations in the area.
