Predjama Castle (Predjamski Grad) Tours and Tickets

2,667 reviews

Built into the mouth of a cave in the middle of a towering limestone cliff, the Renaissance-style Predjama Castle is one of Slovenia’s most popular attractions. The castle is now a museum featuring an eerie dungeon, many 16th-century treasures, and a secret hiding spot called Erazem’s Nook, after Erazem Lueger—Slovenia’s Robin Hood—who hid out from the authorities here.

  • Open Daily 10am - 4pm. Hours extended in summer
  • Predjama 1, Predjama, 6230
