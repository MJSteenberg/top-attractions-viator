Q:How much does it cost to visit York Minster in 2024?
A:

York Minster tickets currently start at €18 for adults, which grants access to the minster only. Prices increase if you want to access or climb the Central Tower—expect to pay closer to €24 to visit the top of the tower. Children 17 and under are free with a paying adult for minster access only.

Q:What is the history of York Minster?
A:

York Minster, one of the largest Gothic cathedrals in Northern Europe, dates back to the 7th century, though the current Gothic structure was initiated in 1220. York Minster has witnessed its fair share of history, including fires, the looting of the Reformation, and the English Civil War. Despite all this, it still features artifacts like the east window, the world's largest expanse of medieval stained glass.

Q:What are the best times to visit York Minster?
A:

The best times to visit York Minster are early morning or late afternoon on weekdays, as this avoids the largest crowds. Visiting during the shoulder seasons of spring (April to June) and autumn (September to October) is also smart, as the weather is pleasant, but tourist numbers are down. Special services and events like Evensong can provide a unique experience, but check the cathedral’s schedule in advance for closures.

Q:What other attractions are near York Minster?
A:

York Minster is located in the heart of York, drowning in visible history. Don’t miss The Shambles, a historic street with overhanging timber-framed buildings; York Castle Museum and the Jorvik Viking Centre, which both bring York’s (Viking) history to life; and the York city walls, which offer great views alongside real-life, tangible medieval history.

Q:Can you climb the tower at York Minster?
A:

Yes, with tower tickets, visitors can climb the Central Tower at York Minster for great views of the city of York and the surrounding countryside. The climb consists of 275 steps up a narrow, winding staircase, requiring a reasonable fitness level. Visitors must be 8 years or older to climb the tower.
