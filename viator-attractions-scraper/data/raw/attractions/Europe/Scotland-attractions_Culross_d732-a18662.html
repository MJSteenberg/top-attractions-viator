Culross Tours and Tickets

3,460 reviews

The Fife village of Culross is easily recognizable for its appearances in the TV series Outlander, but is much more than just a pretty backdrop. Cared for by the National Trust for Scotland, Culross (pronounced “coo-riss”) is one of Scotland’s best-preserved 17th-century royal burghs, with a timeless atmosphere that offers a glimpse into the past.
