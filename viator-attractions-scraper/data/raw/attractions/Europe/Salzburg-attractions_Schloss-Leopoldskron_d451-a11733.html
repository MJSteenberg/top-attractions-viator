Schloss Leopoldskron Tours and Tickets

Built in 1736, Schloss Leopoldskron is a rococo palace that stands within large, manicured grounds on the fringes of Salzburg. The lakeside palace is today run as a hotel and remains instantly recognizable to many as the Von Trapp home in the 1965 movie The Sound of Music.
