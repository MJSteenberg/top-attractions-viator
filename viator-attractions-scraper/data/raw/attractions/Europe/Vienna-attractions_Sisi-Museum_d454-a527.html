Sisi Museum Tours and Tickets

1,663 reviews

See an intimate side of Empress Elisabeth of Austria—a fascinating 19th-century monarch often known as Sisi—at a museum that covers her groundbreaking life and death. The personal items on display here include regal clothing, jewelry, and accessories in exhibits inspired by Sisi’s own poetry.

  • Open daily Sept - June 9am - 5:30pm, July - Aug 9am - 6pm
  • Michaelerkuppel, Vienna, Vienna, 1010
