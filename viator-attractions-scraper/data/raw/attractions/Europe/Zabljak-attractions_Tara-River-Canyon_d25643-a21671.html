Tara River Canyon Tours and Tickets

344 reviews

Snaking through Montenegro’s lush Durmitor National Park, the 94-mile (151-kilometer UNESCO-listed Tara River Canyon is Europe’s deepest gorge and one of its longest. Dramatic walls, turquoise waters, and bordering pine-coated hills make it a magnet for nature lovers and adventure sports enthusiasts.
