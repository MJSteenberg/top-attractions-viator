Curacao (Willemstad) Cruise Port Tours and Tickets

Located off the coast of Venezuela, Curacao is a tiny island nestled in crystal-blue waters and Curacao (Willemstad) Cruise Port serves as its gateway. Once the center of Caribbean commerce, Curacao retains influences from its of French, Dutch, and Portuguese colonial past, by way of its food, architecture, and culture.

Q:How do I get from the Willemstad Cruise Port to downtown Willemstad?
A:

You can reach downtown Willemstad from Willemstad Cruise Port on foot. It's a 10- to 15-minute walk to reach areas like Punda and Otrobanda. The port area has pedestrian-friendly paths, making walking easy. Taxis, shuttle services, buses, and private tours also provide transportation from the port to downtown.

Q:What are the top attractions near the Willemstad Cruise Port in Curaçao?
A:

The Willemstad Cruise Port is near several must-see attractions, including the Queen Emma Bridge, a short walk from the port. The historic Punda district has colorful Dutch colonial buildings, quaint shops, cafés, and galleries. The Floating Market in Punda is where vendors sell fresh produce, seafood, and crafts from boats.

Q:What is the best time to visit Willemstad Cruise Port in Curaçao?
A:

The best time to visit is in the dry season, from December to April. The warm weather makes it ideal for outdoor activities like beach visits, snorkeling, and exploring Willemstad. Expect larger crowds in the dry season, especially around holidays like Christmas, New Year's, and Easter.

Q:Are there beaches close to the Willemstad Cruise Port?
A:

Yes, several beaches are close to the Willemstad Cruise Port—all within a short drive—such as Mambo Beach, one of the most popular beaches in Curaçao; it’s about 10-15 minutes by car from the port. Others include Avila Beach, Parasasa Beach, and Blue Bay Beach.

Q:Are there transportation options available from the Willemstad Cruise Port to nearby attractions?
A:

Taxis are available at the cruise port and are the most convenient option for reaching attractions. Confirm the fare before getting into the cab. Cruise lines offer shuttle services from the port to popular areas, beaches, and shopping centers in Willemstad. Tour operators offer excursions that typically include transportation.

Q:Is there shopping available at or near the Willemstad Cruise Port?
A:

Yes. Within the cruise terminal, souvenir shops offer handicrafts, artisan products, T-shirts, jewelry, and Curaçao-themed souvenirs. A short walk from the port, Punda is Willemstad's main shopping district with boutiques, art galleries, and duty-free shops. Handelskade, a colorful street lined with Dutch colonial buildings, is a top Punda shopping area.
