Q:How much does it cost to visit Bob Marley’s Nine Mile in 2024?
A:

The price for admission to Bob Marley’s Nine Mile in Jamaica starts at about US$30 for a guided tour of the birthplace and mausoleum. Some guided tours from Ocho Rios include roundtrip transportation and cost around US$80. Tours that also include other attractions start around US$130.

Q:How do I get to Bob Marley’s Nine Mile from Montego Bay?
A:

The easiest way to get to Bob Marley’s Nine Mile from Montego Bay is with a guided tour that includes roundtrip transportation and admission. You can rent a car or taxi, but be aware that the 2-hour journey via the A1 highway can be narrow, winding, and filled with potholes.

Q:What can I see and do at Bob Marley’s Nine Mile?
A:

At Bob Marley’s Nine Mile, you can visit the singer’s birthplace and childhood home, see his final resting place (and his mother’s tomb), and view artifacts and memorabilia related to his career. Guided tours provide insights into his life, music, and Rastafarian culture. Local musicians often play Marley’s songs.

Q:Are there any nearby attractions to visit after touring Bob Marley’s Nine Mile?
A:

Yes, a visit to Bob Marley’s Nine Mile is often combined with a stop at Dunn’s River Falls where you can climb the cascades and enjoy the scenery. Green Grotto Caves are limestone caves with interesting rock formations and underground lakes. Both attractions are about one hour from Nine Mile.

Q:Are there any facilities available at Bob Marley’s Nine Mile?
A:

Yes, there are basic facilities at Bob Marley’s Nine Mile including restrooms, a small café with refreshments, and a gift shop selling souvenirs. Parking is available on-site; drive to the blue doors where a security guard will let you inside. You’ll also encounter street vendors outside selling their wares.
