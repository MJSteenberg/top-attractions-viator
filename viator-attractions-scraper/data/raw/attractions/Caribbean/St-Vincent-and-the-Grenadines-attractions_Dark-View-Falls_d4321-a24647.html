Tumbling down from a sheer cliff face into natural pools, Dark View Falls in St. Vincent is spectacular. It comprises two powerful water streams set against a lush green rainforest. Moreover, the waterfalls are easily accessible via a short hike from the welcome center via a rustic bamboo bridge.
