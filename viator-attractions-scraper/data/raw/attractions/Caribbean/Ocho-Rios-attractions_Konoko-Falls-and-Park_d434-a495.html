Konoko Falls and Park Tours and Tickets

High above the sun-drenched beaches and bustling fray of Ocho Rios, the lush Konoko Falls and Park are filled with innumerable species of tropical flora and birds, and streams that tumble into gentle waterfalls. The excellent on-site museum traces the history and culture of Jamaica’s original inhabitants, the Tainos and Arawaks.

Q:How much does it cost to visit Konoko Falls and Park in 2025?
A:

The current entry prices for Konoko Falls and Park in Ocho Rios are US$20 for adults, US$10 for children aged 3–12 years old, and kids under 3 are free. Guided tours that include the falls start at around US$40 and typically visit multiple sights. If joining a tour, entrance fees are sometimes included in the tour price.

Q:Can I visit Konoko Falls with children or people with disabilities?
A:

Konoko Falls and Park is a family-friendly attraction with a botanical garden, small zoo, and aviary with exotic birds. It has a wheelchair-accessible entrance, parking lot, and restrooms. There are also platforms where you can access a portion of the river and see a view of Ocho Rios and the harbor.

Q:What activities are available at Konoko Falls and Park?
A:

At Konoko Falls and Park, you can enjoy a variety of activities, including climbing the Konoko Falls and swimming in its pools. There’s an on-site museum showcasing the island's heritage, plus botanical gardens, an aviary, and a small zoo.

Q:Are there any nearby attractions to visit after Konoko Falls and Park?
A:

After visiting Konoko Falls and Park, you can explore nearby attractions such as the Dunn's River Falls and Park, where you can climb the waterfall. The Ocho Rios Blue Hole, a beautiful natural swimming spot, is also close by. Additionally, you can visit Mystic Mountain, where you can enjoy activities like a bobsled ride, ziplining, and a chairlift.​

Q:How long should I plan to spend at Konoko Falls and Park?
A:

You should expect to spend around 1–2 hours at Konoko Falls and Park. This will allow you time to explore the botanical gardens, hike to the waterfall, enjoy the zoo, and visit the museum. If you plan to take a dip in the pools, be sure to allocate extra time to change.
