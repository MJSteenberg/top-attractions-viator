Booby Cay Island Tours and Tickets

258 reviews

A small, uninhabited island off the northern coast of Jamaica near Negril, Booby Cay Island is named after the booby birds that live and nest there. Known for its white sand beach and clear waters ideal for swimming, snorkeling, and scuba diving, the island is a popular day-trip destination.
