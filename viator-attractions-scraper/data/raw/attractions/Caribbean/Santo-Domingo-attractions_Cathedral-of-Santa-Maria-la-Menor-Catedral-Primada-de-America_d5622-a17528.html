Cathedral of Santa Maria la Menor (Catedral Primada de América) Tours and Tickets

4,452 reviews
Free admission

Built in the early 16th century, the Cathedral of Santa Maria la Menor (Catedral Primada de América) is the oldest Catholic cathedral in the New World. Located in the Colonial Zone, the cathedral is one of Santo Domingo’s most famous religious sites, and contains a large collection of woodcarving and religious artifacts.

  • Callejon de los Curas, Santo Domingo, 10210
