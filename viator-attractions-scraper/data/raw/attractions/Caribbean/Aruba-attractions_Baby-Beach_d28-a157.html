Q:What is Baby Beach on Aruba known for?
A:

Baby Beach on Aruba is considered one of the most popular beaches for kids in the Caribbean, thanks to its warm, shallow waters. Q:What is the best time of year to visit Baby Beach on Aruba?

Baby Beach on Aruba can get very crowded when the cruise ships are in port and on the weekends when locals head here. Consider visiting early in the morning on a weekday to avoid crowds. Aruba is a year-round destination, with January to March being the most popular time to visit.

Q:How do I get to Baby Beach from the main tourist areas on Aruba?
