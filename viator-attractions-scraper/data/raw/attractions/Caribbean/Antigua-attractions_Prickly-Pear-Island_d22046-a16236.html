Prickly Pear Island Tours and Tickets

This tiny islet off Antigua plays the part of an uninhabited island paradise to a T. Ringed by coral reefs with a white sandy beach, Prickly Pear Island gives visitors plenty of space to relax on lounge chairs while sipping tropical cocktails from the bar. Or, spend the afternoon snorkeling in its clear blue waters.
