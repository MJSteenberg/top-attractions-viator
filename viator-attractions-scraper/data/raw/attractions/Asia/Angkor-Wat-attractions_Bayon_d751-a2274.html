Bayon Tours and Tickets

The geographical and spiritual center of the ancient city of Angkor Thom, the Bayon is one of the crowning artistic achievements of the Khmer king Jayavarman VII. Around 200 giant faces look down from around 50 towers, while beautifully crafted reliefs depict everyday life in 12th-century Cambodia.

My tour guide (Dan) was terrific! He was a good storyteller and had a vast knowledge of Cambodian history (both cultural and political) as well as the religions that shaped the building of the temples and the carvings on the walls. He also augmented his discussions with pictures and videos. For instance, one of the relief sculptures on the wall depicted a Hindu battle scene. We were fortunate that it was reasonably mild weather that morning (85 degrees) so it wasnt as taxing as it may be in warmer weather. I highly recommend this tour.
