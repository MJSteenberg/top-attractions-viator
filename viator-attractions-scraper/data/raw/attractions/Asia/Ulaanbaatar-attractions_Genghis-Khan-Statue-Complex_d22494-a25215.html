Genghis Khan Statue Complex (Chinggis Khaan Statue Complex) Tours and Tickets

582 reviews

By far Mongolia’s most recognizable landmark and one of the world’s largest equestrian statues, the 131-foot-tall (40-meter-tall) Genghis Khan Statue Complex (Chinggis Khaan Statue Complex) towers over the surrounding landscape. Ride the elevator up inside the horse, learn about the Mongols at the museum, shop for souvenirs, or enjoy a snack at the café.

Q:How much does it cost to visit the Genghis Khan Statue Complex (Chinggis Khaan Statue Complex) in 2025?
A:

Entrance to the Genghis Khan Statue Complex is free. Still, it’s worth booking a tour that includes round-trip transportation from Ulaanbaatar and exploration of the Gorkhi-Terelj National Park. Tours typically start at around US$45, and some include lunch, horseback riding, and camel rides, with according price increases.

Q:When is the best time to visit the Genghis Khan Statue Complex (Chinggis Khaan Statue Complex)?
A:

The statue complex is open year-round during daylight hours. Check with your local contact in Ulaanbaatar for the exact times from morning to evening. The best months to visit Mongolia are from May to October, when days are long, and temperatures are moderate. Winter is generally too cold.

Q:Can you visit the Genghis Khan Statue Complex (Chinggis Khaan Statue Complex) by yourself without a guided tour?
A:

Yes, you can visit the Genghis Khan Statue Complex (Chinggis Khaan Statue Complex) on your own, but then you have to arrange your transportation, which can be a hassle. It’s best to hire a driver or join a private tour or group tour that includes round-trip transportation.

Q:How much time should I spend at the Genghis Khan Statue Complex (Chinggis Khaan Statue Complex)?
A:

Plan to spend around an hour or more to tour the statue complex, but allow nearly a full day for a tour of the complex and the Gorkhi-Terelj National Park. Day trip activities often include shooting arrows on horseback, riding camels, and eating a traditional Mongolian lunch.

Q:Is the Genghis Khan Statue Complex (Chinggis Khaan Statue Complex) worth it?
A:

Yes, the 1.5-hour drive from Ulaanbaatar to the Genghis Khan Statue Complex is well worth it. Ghenghis Khan is Mongolia’s hero, and this monument honors him and other past khans of the region. Here, you can learn about Mongolian history and culture and buy souvenirs.
