Mollem National Park Tours and Tickets

Located in the Western Ghats mountains of eastern Goa, Mollem National Park is a forested reserve that’s home to a huge range of Indian wildlife and flora. The national park sits within the larger Bhagwan Mahavir Wildlife Sanctuary, which runs along the border of Karnataka state. Mollem is popular for trekking and spotting leopards, tigers, pangolins, black panthers, and Indian bison.
