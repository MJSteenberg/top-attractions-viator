Maruyama Park (Maruyama Koen) Tours and Tickets

1,670 reviews
Free admission

When Sapporo residents want to relax, play, or soak up nature, they head for Maruyama Park (Maruyama Koen). On the western side of the city, the park’s sprawling grounds include the Maruyama Wild Forest, a designated natural treasure, and several attractions including the Hokkaido Shrine, the Maruyama Zoo, and the Maruyama Baseball Stadium.

  • 473 Maruyamacho, Higashiyama-Ku, Kyoto, 605-0071
