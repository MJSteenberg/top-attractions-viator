Klungkung (Semarapura) Tours and Tickets

1,922 reviews

Despite being renamed Semarapura, Klungkung, once the capital of a powerful Balinese kingdom, is still generally known by its historic name. A bustling little district capital, it’s home to a number of relics of the Klungkung kingdom as well as a market that’s a popular place to shop for weaving, particularlyikat andsongket.
