Omotesando Tours and Tickets

Omotesando, the elegant, tree-lined avenue between Shibuya and Minato wards, is Tokyo’s answer to the grand thoroughfares of Paris. And, just like the Champs-Élysées, Omotesando is lined with high-end shopping, with flagship stores from brands including Louis Vuitton, Dior, and Gucci, as well as the sprawling shopping complex of Omotesando Hills.
