Suzhou Museum Tours and Tickets

28 reviews
Free admission

Located in Suzhou’s historic quarter, Suzhou Museum was designed by famed architect I.M. Pei. It’s a masterful blend of modern design, traditional Suzhou architectural style, and classical garden layout, and the museum’s light-filled galleries house a collection of bronze, jade, ceramics, porcelain, handicrafts, paintings, calligraphies, and relics.

  • 204 Dongbei St., Gusu District, Suzhou, Jiangsu, 215005
