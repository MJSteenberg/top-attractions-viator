Padar Island Tours and Tickets

1,690 reviews

One of the smaller islands in Komodo National Park, Padar Island’s rugged green promontories, white sand beaches, and turquoise waters make social media magic. A hike to the island-top viewpoint is an essential stop on most Komodo National Park tours, while one of the park’s pink beaches is tucked into a bay on the north coast.
