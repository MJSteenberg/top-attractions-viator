Q:How much does it cost to visit Leisure World Water Park in 2025?
A:

Day passes for Leisure World Water Park can range from around 2,500 Sri Lankan rupees to 3,750 Sri Lankan rupees for access to its water park and amusement park areas. Special deals and discounts may be available, so check with the park directly for up-to-date prices and options.

Q:What is the best time to visit Leisure World Water Park?
A:

The best time to visit Leisure World Water Park is during southwest Sri Lanka’s dry season (December–March), when warm weather and clear skies make visiting extra enjoyable. If possible, come to the park on weekdays and early in the day to avoid the busy afternoons and weekends.

Q:Are there lockers available at Leisure World Water Park?
A:

Yes, there are locker facilities available at Leisure World Water Park. You can rent lockers for a fee to stow your non-swim clothes and other belongings safely while you enjoy the park’s attractions and rides. Other facilities at the park include changing rooms, restrooms, ATMs, and parking.

Q:Can I bring my own food and drink into Leisure World Water Park?
A:

No, you may not bring your own food and drink into Leisure World Water Park—although you can take in baby food for infants. There are numerous restaurants and food outlets at the park where you can buy snacks, meals, and drinks.

Q:Is Leisure World Water Park good for young kids?
A:

Yes, Leisure World Water Park is great for young kids, with attractions suitable for children at both its water park and amusement areas. Water park attractions for little ones include the junior slides, pirate ship, and kiddie pool. The amusement park offers a mini-train, mini-coaster, and carousels.
