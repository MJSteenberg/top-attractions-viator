Q:How much does it cost to visit Similan Islands National Park in 2024?
A:

It costs THB 500 for foreign adults to visit Similan Islands National Park and THB 250 for foreign children aged 3 to 14. Thai citizens pay THB 100 for adults and THB 50 for children. Kids under three years of age visit free, irrespective of citizenship.

Q:Whatâ€™s the best way to experience Similan Islands National Park?
A:

The only way to visit the Similan Islands National Park is by joining a tour, and there are strict restrictions on the number of tourists allowed in the park at any given time due to problems with overtourism. Most tours depart from Khao Lak or Phuket.

Q:What is the best month to visit the Similan Islands?
A:

The best time to visit the Similan Islands is between December and March, when conditions are at their best for snorkeling and diving. Note that Similan Islands National Park is only open from the middle of October until the middle of May (this is to help curb overtourism).

Q:What facilities are available at Similan Islands National Park?
A:

Facilities in the Similan Islands are limited, so bring everything you need for the day, including your swimsuit, sun hat, towels, and reef-safe sunscreen. Many tours provide lunch and bottled water, but it's smart to check in advance just to be sure, and you may want to bring snacks either way.

Q:What activities can be done at Similan Islands National Park?
A:

The most popular activities at Similan Islands National Park include snorkeling and diving, and many people visit specifically because they want to get out in the waters and see aquatic creatures such as moray eels and leopard sharks. Swimming and relaxing on white-sand beaches are also popular things to do.
