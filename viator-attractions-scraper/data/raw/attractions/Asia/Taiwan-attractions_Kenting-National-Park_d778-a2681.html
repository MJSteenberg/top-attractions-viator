Kenting National Park Tours and Tickets

716 reviews

Located at the southern tip of Taiwan on the Hengchun Peninsula, Kenting National Park is home to some of the country’s loveliest white sand beaches. It’s also a popular destination for divers, thanks to its coral reefs and marine life, and hikers, who frequent its scenic mountains. Kenting Town is a primary jumping-off point for a visit.
