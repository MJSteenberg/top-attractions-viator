San Felipe de Barajas Castle (Castillo San Felipe de Barajas) Tours and Tickets

2,376 reviews

Some historians say that if it weren’t for San Felipe de Barajas Castle (Castillo San Felipe de Barajas), South America would now speak English. The 14th-century fortress protected the coastal city of Cartagena from English invasion, allowing the Spanish to maintain their rule. Besides the role it plays in Colombia’s history, the castle attracts visitors with its panoramic harbor views.

  • Avenida Antonio de Arévalo Carrera 17 Calel 32 No. 17-85, Barrio Pie del Cerro, Cartagena, Bolivar

Q:Where is San Felipe de Barajas Castle located, and how do I get there?
A:
The San Felipe de Barajas Castle is on top of San Lazaro Hill, east of the Getsemani neighborhood. You can walk across the bridge from the Getsemani neighborhood. Several buses stop nearby, including the hop-on, hop-off sightseeing bus. Taxis are an easy option. Many sightseeing tours also include a stop at the fortress.
Q:What are San Felipe de Barajas Castle's opening hours and days of operation, and is there an entrance fee?
A:
The San Felipe de Barajas Castle is open from 7am to 6pm, Tuesday through Sunday. There is an entrance fee, with discounts for children between 6 and 13 and students. Free entry is offered for children under 6 and visitors with disabilities and for Colombian nationals on the last Sunday of the month from February to November.
Q:How much time do I need for San Felipe de Barajas Castle?
A:
How much time you need depends on your level of interest. The fortress is large but can be explored in less than an hour. Allow more time to explore the underground tunnels. The audio guide offers three different programs (60, 90, and 180 minutes), depending on your time.
Q:What is the best time of the day to visit San Felipe de Barajas Castle?
A:
It’s best to visit San Felipe de Barajas Castle early in the morning to beat the crowds and the heat. Another good time to visit is around 4 pm. That should give you plenty of time to explore the fortress and enjoy sunset views over the city.
Q:Are there guided tours available at San Felipe de Barajas Castle, and how can I book them?
A:
Yes, guided tours are available at San Felipe de Barajas Castle. You can book them at the entrance, where you purchase tickets. A paid audio guide is also available, allowing you to explore at your own pace. Many guided Cartagena sightseeing tours also include San Felipe de Barajas Castle.
Q:What are some tips for visiting San Felipe de Barajas Castle to make the most of my experience?
A:
What are some tips for visiting San Felipe de Barajas Castle to make the most of my experience? For a more enjoyable experience, visit earlier in the morning or the evening to beat the heat. Wear comfortable shoes, wear sun protection, and bring water, as there is limited shade, and you will be walking uphill. A guided tour or audio guide can help enrich your visit.
