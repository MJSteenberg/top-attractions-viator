Santos Coffee Museum (Museu do Café) Tours and Tickets

244 reviews

At the Santos Coffee Museum (Museu do Café), you can discover the history of the world’s favorite caffeinated-beverage. The Coffee Museum is housed in what used to be the Coffee Stock Exchange, where Brazilian coffee was weighed and traded before being sent through the Santos Port and overseas.

  • Rua Quinze de Novembro 95, Centro, Santos, São Paulo, 11010-150
