Curi-Cancha Reserve Tours and Tickets

The cloud forest of Curi-Cancha Reserve creates a habitat for flora and fauna such as colorful quetzals and monkeys. Visitors can explore the private reserve via several nature trails. Plus, the private reserve’s daily 50 person cap means you’ll see more wildlife without having to battle the crowds.

Q:How much does it cost to visit the Curi-Cancha Reserve in 2024?
A:

You can explore the Curi-Cancha Reserve by yourself, by booking a tour through the reserve, or by booking a private or group tour that may include transport and additional stops. Entry without a guide is US$20 for adults and US$12 for kids aged 5-17. Prices increase if you book one of the reserve’s tours: choose from birdwatching, natural history, or night tours. Tours booked outside the reserve can hit up to around US$225 per person for a private tour.

Q:When is the best time of day to visit the Curi-Cancha Reserve?
A:

The best time of day to visit the Curi-Cancha Reserve is early in the morning when the reserve opens—typically at 7am—to see the most activity. Another popular time is in the evening when the nocturnal creatures are active, but you’ll need to join a night tour to do this.

Q:When is the best month to visit the Curi-Cancha Reserve?
A:

If your main focus is birdwatching, head to Curi-Cancha Reserve in the dry months—around December to April—when you’re likely to see the most migratory birds and resident species. However, the reserve is open year-round and you’ll see birds any time.

Q:Is the Curi-Cancha Reserve worth it?
A:

Yes, if you’re a birdwatcher, then the Curi-Cancha Reserve is worth it. Even if you’re not particularly into birds, the other species in the area are remarkable. Guides can help elevate your experience and help you spot the wildlife.

Q:Is the Curi-Cancha Reserve good for kids?
A:

Yes, the Curi-Cancha Reserve is great for the entire family. Kids get to see all types of birds, several species of monkeys, sloths, armadillos, and much more. They need to have patience, though: this isn’t a zoo with animals in cages.
