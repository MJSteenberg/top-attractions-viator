Paseo 21 de Mayo Tours and Tickets

2,024 reviews

Hailed as Valparaiso's most picturesque passage—and nicknamed the Balcony of Valparaiso—Paseo 21 de Mayo winds through street-art smothered neighborhoods and the colorful houses of Cerro Playa Ancha. Its elevated location also gives you sweeping panoramas of Valparaiso's UNESCO-listed historical center and the ocean beyond it.
