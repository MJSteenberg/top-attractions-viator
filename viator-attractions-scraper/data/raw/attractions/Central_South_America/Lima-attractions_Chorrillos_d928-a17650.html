Chorrillos Tours and Tickets

South of its more famous neighbors, Miraflores and Barranco, the Chorrillos district is a charming off-the-beaten path adventure. The small fishing village, once a beach resort to Lima’s wealthiest denizens, boasts lovely beaches and green parks, an astronomical observatory and planetarium, wetlands that house hundreds of species of birds, and an embankment with a stunning view of Lima bay.
