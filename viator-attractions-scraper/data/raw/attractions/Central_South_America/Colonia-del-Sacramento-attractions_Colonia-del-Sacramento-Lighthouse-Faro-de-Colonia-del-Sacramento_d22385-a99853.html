Colonia del Sacramento Lighthouse (Faro de Colonia del Sacramento) Tours and Tickets

The slender white tower that rises over Colonia del Sacramento’s southwest shore is one of the city’s most important historical monuments. Dating back to the mid-19th century, the landmark lighthouse replaced a 17th-century colonial church tower, taking over the role of guiding ships through the notoriously treacherous waters of the Rio de la Plata.
