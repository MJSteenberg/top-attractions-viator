Baldi Hot Springs Tours and Tickets

569 reviews

Located in the shadow of active Arenal Volcano and surrounded by a lush jungle landscape, Baldi Hot Springs is one of the largest hot springs resorts in the world. Visitors can choose from 25 different thermal pools, plus pampering spa treatments, swim-up bars, and Costa Rican cuisine for a complete day of relaxation.

  • Daily 10 a.m.-10 p.m.
  • San Carlos, Alajuela, 30109
